Move, Collect, Get Kill, Upgrade, Survive

========================

MOVE : ASDW / arrow key

Weapons : 1-9 / mouse rotate

Shoot : left mouse

Shield : E (cannot shoot)

Pickup : F

Reload : R

========================

Hide/Open minimap: M

Change view: V (camara do not track player)

Switch player want to track (after you death): Click

View minimap: Q (Hold mouse, move to position you want to see on the minimap)

========================

Hide/Open Chat: C

Add message: ENTER

Change Music: N

Help: H

Pause / Continue: ESC

========================

More Tip:

+ Shoot to tree will have blood and change of having a weapon

+ Destroy rock will have powerful weapon

+ When you have more than 10 killed, you can go outside of map. Go to another map
